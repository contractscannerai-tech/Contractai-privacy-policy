Privacy Policy for ContractAI

Effective Date: April 14, 2026

ContractAI (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

  • Email address
  • Full name
  • Password (securely encrypted or handled by authentication provider)
  • Google account information (if you sign in using Google)

We do not collect phone numbers or unnecessary personal data.

2. DOCUMENT UPLOADS

Users may upload PDF contracts and images of contracts. Uploaded documents are used solely for analysis purposes.

3. HOW WE PROCESS DOCUMENTS

  • Uploaded documents are processed using AI systems for analysis.
  • Images are converted to text using an OCR (Optical Character Recognition) API.
  • The full document content is sent to our AI system for processing.
  • After analysis is completed, uploaded files are automatically deleted from our system within approximately 30 seconds.

4. AI PROCESSING

We use third-party AI services (such as GROQ API) to analyze uploaded contracts. The AI processes the full document to generate summaries, risk detection, and key clauses. We do not permanently store raw document content after processing.

5. DATA STORAGE

We store User account information (via Supabase), contract analysis results, and contract names. We do NOT permanently store uploaded documents.

6. DATA RETENTION

  • Uploaded documents: deleted within ~30 seconds after processing.
  • User account data: stored until the user deletes their account.
  • Analysis history: stored while the account remains active.

7. USER CONTROL

Users can delete their account at any time. When an account is deleted, all associated data is also removed.

8. DATA SECURITY

We use Supabase and secure backend systems with encrypted authentication and secure database storage.

9. DATA SHARING

We do not sell user data. Limited data is shared strictly for functionality with: GROQ API (AI), OCR API (Text), and Dodo Payments.

10. EMAIL COMMUNICATION

We only use email for support and account notifications. We do not send marketing emails.

11. ACCOUNT REQUIREMENT

Users must create an account to save analysis history and secure user data.

12. LEGAL DISCLAIMER

ContractAI provides AI-generated insights for informational purposes only. It does NOT provide legal advice.

13. LOCATION DATA

We may collect IP-based location data via Dodo Payments for tax calculation based on region.
